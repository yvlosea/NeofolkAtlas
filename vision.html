Neofolk Atlas Learning as Identity v0.6.2 Alpha

Vision

Neofolk Humanitarian Board of Education

Neofolk Atlas is designed as an academic network where identity is shaped by disciplined study, documented work, and long-form inquiry rather than by passive credential collection.

Portfolio-Based Education

Learning is evidenced through records of work, reflection, documentation, and practical contribution rather than one-time examinations alone.

Guild Research Structure

Guilds are research collectives where seekers and curators can investigate questions, produce outputs, and submit them for arbiter review.

Studio Environments

Studios represent capability environments unlocked through demonstrated contribution, readiness, and intellectual seriousness.

Infrastructure

Nodes, review, and future expansion

The longer-term vision is a decentralised network of educational nodes with clear review structures, interoperable portfolios, and scholarly pathways that can grow without collapsing into a single rigid institution.

Subjects

Structured domains for foundational study and literacy.

Guilds

Collaborative environments for research, experimentation, and documented inquiry.

Arbiters

Academic reviewers who approve modules, assess outputs, and protect standards.
