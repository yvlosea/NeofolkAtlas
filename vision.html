Neofolk

Vision

Neofolk Humanitarian Board of Education

Neofolk Atlas is designed as an academic network where identity is shaped by disciplined study, documented work, and long-form inquiry rather than by passive credential collection. The platform mirrors a private academic archive, focusing on structured reading, rigorous peer evaluation, and a collective repository of intellectual artifacts.

Core Idea

Learning is evidenced through records of work, reflection, documentation, and practical contribution rather than one-time examinations alone. It is about establishing long-term intellectual records.

Domain Model

The academic universe is divided into 10 structured domains representing a comprehensive survey of knowledge: Lingosophy, Arthmetics, Cosmology, Biosphere, Chronicles, Civitas, Tokenomics, Artifex, Praxis, and Bioepisteme.

Guild Structure

Guilds are research collectives where seekers and curators can investigate interconnected questions across domains, produce joint outputs, and submit them for arbiter review.

Nodes

Nodes are independent educational units interconnected throughout the network. They provide local leadership within the universal framework.

Studios

Studios represent capability environments unlocked through demonstrated contribution, readiness, and serious intellectual production.

Atheneums

Spaces dedicated strictly to deep archiving, reading, and contemplative philosophy, ensuring the preservation of the network's knowledge artifacts.

Coliseums

Areas meant for rigorous debate, public defense of theses, and competitive intellectual discourse.

Panarchium

The governing collective structure that unites the disparate entities under a single ethical and architectural charter.

Travel Learning Infrastructure

Supporting infrastructure, including Neobuses and Lodges, allows physical convergence and travel across nodes, ensuring that learning transcends digital environments.
