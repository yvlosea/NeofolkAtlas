Fund Allocation Plan

How donations and funding will be utilized across the project lifecycle.

50% Construction & Infrastructure
25% Educational Programs & Faculty
15% Operations & Scholarships
10% Reserve & Sustainability

Construction & Infrastructure (50%)

Building the Library Tower, hostels, kitchen, and campus facilities. Includes materials, labor, and professional fees.

Educational Programs (25%)

Faculty salaries, curriculum development, workshop materials, and learning resources.

Operations & Scholarships (15%)

Daily running costs, student meals, maintenance, and scholarships for underprivileged students.

Reserve Fund (10%)

Emergency reserves and sustainability fund for long-term operational stability.
