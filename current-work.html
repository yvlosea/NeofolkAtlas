India has no shortage of classrooms. What it lacks is an education system that treats students as complete human beings.

The Beno Tower

Hexagonal Learning Structure

A climate-responsive hexagonal tower designed as the visual identity and flagship hub of the campus. Features passive cooling, natural ventilation, and a coloured pergola roof.
