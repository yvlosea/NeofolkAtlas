Site Survey Complete

Initial land survey and soil testing completed at Panchmahala. Results confirm suitability for construction. Boundary markers established and initial site documentation photographed.

Design January 2025

Library Tower Design Finalized

After months of iteration, the circular Library Tower design is complete. Climate-responsive features integrated: cross-ventilation, passive cooling, rainwater harvesting, and solar-ready infrastructure.

Panchmahala Community Consultation

Met with village residents and panchayat members to discuss the project. Strong local support expressed. Community input incorporated into campus planning, especially regarding access roads and water sharing.

Campus Masterplan v1.0
