Strategic Positioning

Why Panchmahala?

Location choice is strategic. This area can become an education hub.

Proximity to Patna

90km from state capital. Accessible for partnerships, talent recruitment, and government liaison.

Rail Access via Mokama

Mokama Junction on Delhi-Howrah main line. Direct connectivity to major metros.

Ganges Proximity

Cultural significance, water access, spiritual anchor for holistic education.

Family-Held Land

18 Katha family land available at sustainable cost. No speculative pressure.

Township Expansion

Bihar government planning infrastructure expansion. Early-mover advantage.

Education Vacuum

No quality residential educational institutions in 50km radius. Underserved community.

Concept Inspiration & Goal

Solarpunk, hope, and a future built with nature

Our idea is based on the solarpunk eco-futurism idea, where technology works with nature not against it. The solarpunk revolution is a need for the Indian growing economy to provide self sustenince, walkable architecture and community travel support. Our goal starts with the school, but it dosen't stop until India is a nation that is again renowned for its sustainability, comfort and quality education. We look to the sun and ask for a future where to the youth, the stars are also visible.

Inspiration reference: "Dear Alice" by THE LINE on YouTube.
