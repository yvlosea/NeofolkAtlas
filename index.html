Current Work

Panchmahala is the clearest public expression of what we are building.

The homepage should make one thing obvious: what iNHET is working on right now. That is the Panchmahala pilot. It brings together library space, studios, classrooms, gardens, and a central learning tower in one coherent educational vision.

This is not meant to feel like a product launch page. It should feel like a real institution explaining its work clearly, with enough visual material to make the project legible and compelling.

Approach

Educational, sustainable, and professional.

We are building a learning environment that values writing, design, discussion, research, problem-solving, and long-term intellectual growth. The website should reflect that seriousness without becoming cold or corporate.

Educational clarity

People should understand the work quickly: what it is, who it serves, and what stage it is in.

Sustainable identity

The visual system should feel green, rooted, calm, and civic rather than flashy or over-designed.

Contact

For partnerships, support, and collaboration.

The public page should make it easy for the right people to reach the trust, ask questions, and understand where to go next.
