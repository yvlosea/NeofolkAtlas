Current Work Live Focus

Learning work with visible form.

The Panchmahala pilot is the main public focus of the platform right now. We want people to see the work, understand the structure, and feel the educational ambition behind it immediately.

The visual language is greener, circular, and spatial: library, studios, tower, amphitheatre, classes, bridges, and gardens working together as one learning ecology.

Library
Grounded public knowledge access with open reading and study spaces.
Tower
A vertical learning landmark for seminars, writing, research, and collective work.
Studios
Creative production zones for curriculum, media, discussion, and practical work.
Gardens
A sustainable site language that keeps the project ecological and humane.
