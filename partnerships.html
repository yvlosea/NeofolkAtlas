Building India's first sustainable student town requires partners. Whether you are a donor, educator, architect, or organization—there is a place for you in this journey.

Donors

Individual donors, HNIs, and foundations who believe in reimagining rural education.

  • One-time or recurring donations
  • Named spaces and scholarships
  • 12A/80G tax benefits (in process)
  • Regular reporting & transparency
