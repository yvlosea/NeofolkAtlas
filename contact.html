Location

Panchmahala, Rural India

Located in Panchmahala, Mokama, Bihar, this pilot institution is designed to serve surrounding communities with accessible, rigorous, sustainable education. The campus is being built on 18 Katha of land (~24,500 square feet) with the ambition to become a model replicable across rural India.
