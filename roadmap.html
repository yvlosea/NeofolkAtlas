Execution Plan

Roadmap

A phased approach to building India's first sustainable student town. From pilot to full ecosystem.

Phase 1

Foundation

2026–2028

Library Tower, basic hostel, kitchen. Pilot launch with 20 students.

→
Phase 2

Expansion

2028–2031

Mobility loop, expanded classrooms, women's programs, sports facilities.

→
Phase 3

Student Town

2031–2036

Full town (200+ residents), research centers, publishing, regional hub.

Foundation (0–2 Years)

Active Planning
🏗️

Library Tower

  • Library tower concept for Panchmahala
  • Climate-responsive design
  • Flexible study and gathering spaces
  • Completion: 2027
🏠

Basic Hostel

  • Residential space for 20 students
  • Shared living areas
  • Dormitory-style accommodation
  • Completion: 2027
🍃

Community Kitchen

  • Meal preparation for residents
  • Nutrition-first menu planning
  • Culinary training space
  • Completion: 2026

NIOS Accreditation

  • Curriculum framework finalized
  • Faculty recruitment
  • Student admissions open: 2026
