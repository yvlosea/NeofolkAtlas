Nutrition & Education

Food Systems

Community kitchen, nutrition-first meal planning, culinary education, and local food entrepreneurship. Garden to table, education to enterprise.

Our Approach

From Garden to Community

🍲

Community Kitchen

Central facility preparing nutritious meals for all residents with nutrition-first planning.

  • 50+ meals daily capacity
  • Balanced nutrition focus
  • Local ingredient sourcing
  • Dietary accommodation
🌾

Kitchen Gardens

On-campus food production teaching sustainable agriculture and providing fresh ingredients.

  • Vegetable cultivation
  • Herb gardens
  • Composting systems
  • Student participation
👩‍🍳

Culinary Training

Practical cooking skills from basics to entrepreneurship, with focus on Bihari cuisine preservation.

  • Cooking fundamentals
  • Traditional recipes
  • Food safety
  • Business basics
