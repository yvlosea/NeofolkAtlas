Phase 1 — Legally Secured
Pilot Execution Zone
Beno Tower
01
Hexagonal learning tower — flagship vertical hub and visual identity of campus
  • Ground Floor: Library, reading spaces, book archives, quiet study corners
  • First Floor: Research rooms, writing spaces, seminar discussions, digital study access
  • Second Floor: Multi-purpose learning rooms, guest lectures, community classes
  • Roof Pavilion: Open-air learning zone, discussion circles, night classes, events, stargazing, creative gatherings
  • Design: hexagonal form, external staircase, coloured pergola roof, passive cooling
Academic Block
02
Structured education centre — operational complement to the Tower
  • Classrooms for core NIOS curriculum
  • Robotics and AI labs
  • Language learning spaces
  • Computer labs and exam spaces
  • Skill development and maker spaces
  • Future vocational training rooms
Hostels
03
Affordable residential accommodation for students and visitors
  • Shared student rooms
  • Visiting faculty accommodation
  • Common lounges and study rooms
  • Laundry areas
  • Terrace recreation and badminton spaces
  • Community kitchens (planned for later phase)
Garden Corridor
04
Green campus connector and outdoor learning environment
  • Walking paths and outdoor study areas
  • Rest areas and meditation zones
  • Shade structures and micro farming
  • Herbology and Ayurvedic plant sections
  • Visual breathing space for the campus
Primary Mobility Path
05
Main pedestrian and mobility spine connecting the campus
  • Walkable pedestrian spine
  • Future electric shuttle movement
  • Bicycle circulation
  • Accessibility-first movement design

Creative Studios

Cultural and creative production spaces supporting the iNHET mission
  • Music rooms and film production spaces

Amphitheatre
07
Public face of the campus — performances, festivals, community events
  • Theatre performances and cultural showcases
  • Festivals and community gatherings
  • Public debates and film screenings
  • Music events and graduation ceremonies
Sports Fields
08
Outdoor athletic and community recreation infrastructure
  • Football, cricket, and athletics
  • Outdoor recreation and community sports
  • Agriculture-tech demonstration space (flexible use)
Swimming Pool
09
Physical development and swimming education facility
  • Swimming education for all students
  • Fitness and student recreation
  • Competitive training possibility in later phases
  • Note: expensive infrastructure — not prioritised in early build
